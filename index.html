Exploring the filling order of Sudoku puzzles
Visualises the steps participants took through Sudoku puzzles. The y-axis indicates the cell in the puzzle and the x-axis is the step at which the cell was filled in. Participants that made mistakes are excluded from this visualisation. Red lines are paths taken through the puzzle by the online solver at https://www.sudokuwiki.org/sudoku.htm .
